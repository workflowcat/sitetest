Building in public is the practice of openly sharing your work, process, and knowledge while creating.

## Why Build in Public?

### Accountability

When others can see your progress, you're more likely to follow through.

### Feedback

Early and frequent feedback helps build better things.

### Community

You attract like-minded people interested in your journey.

### Documentation

Your public trail becomes documentation for your future self.

## What to Share

- **Progress updates** â€” What you're working on
- **Discoveries** â€” What you learned or understood
- **Struggles** â€” Where you're stuck (others can help!)
- **Wins** â€” Celebrate milestones, however small

## What to Keep Private

Not everything belongs in public:

- Personal or sensitive information
- Work under NDA
- Ideas not yet ready for discussion
