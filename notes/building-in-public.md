Building in public means sharing your work, process, and learnings openly as you create.

## Why Build in Public?

### Accountability
When others can see your progress, you're more likely to follow through.

### Feedback
Early and frequent feedback helps you build better things.

### Community
You attract like-minded people interested in your journey.

### Documentation
Your public trail becomes documentation for your future self.

## What to Share

- **Progress updates** - What you're working on
- **Learnings** - What you discovered or figured out
- **Struggles** - Where you're stuck (others may help!)
- **Wins** - Celebrate milestones, however small

## What to Keep Private

Not everything belongs in public:

- Personal or sensitive information
- Work under NDA
- Ideas you're not ready to discuss
- Content that could harm others

## Tips for Getting Started

1. **Start small** - One tweet, one short post
2. **Be consistent** - Regular updates beat viral posts
3. **Be authentic** - Share real struggles, not just wins
4. **Engage** - Reply to comments, thank people
